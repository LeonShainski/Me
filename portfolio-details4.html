ShopSmart

ShopSmart is a chrome web extension I developed alongside a team of three fellow software engineers as part of uOttaHack 5 (2023). This application was developed targeting the Noibu eCommerce challenge, the biggest challenge of the hackathon, which challenged teams to come up with an innovative solution that would help eCommerce developrs, Consumers, and Brands alike (with bonus points for using a chrome extension). This was the first time anyone on the team developed a chrome extension, let alone anything to do with eCommerce. Yet, simply using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (as that is all extensions allow), alongside Bootstrap and a relational database hosted on Supabase, the team was able to create an innovative extension that tracked the first two minutes of a user's interactions and mouse movements on a website. A list of actions the users has taken then becomes available to partner brands and developer, alongside a heatmap of the user's mouse movements throughout the webpage. This allows for correlating what causes the users to eventually become a client, giving brands and developers better visibility into their users' behaviour in an organic manner, whilst also providing benefits to the users as partnered brands are to provide users a kickback coupon via our extension for their data. ShopSmart ended up winning the Noibu challenge at uOttaHack 5.
